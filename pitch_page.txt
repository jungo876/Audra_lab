JUDGES' Q&A CHEAT SHEET

Q: How is this different from other deepfake detectors?
A: We focus on PRIVACY FIRST. Journalists won't upload sensitive
   leaks to a platform that stores data. Our Zero-Knowledge architecture
   solves the trust gap that existing tools miss.

Q: How do you handle high-resolution video?
A: We use LPU (Language Processing Units) for high-throughput frame
   sampling. Zero latency even on 4K inputs.

Q: How do you know if it's a deepfake vs. just low quality?
A: Our engine distinguishes between natural 'Compression Noise' and
   'Generative Artifacts' (synthetic patterns) using ELA.

Q: Is this scalable for newsrooms?
A: Yes. Serverless architecture. Thousands of images per hour with
   zero latency degradation. Look at the Global Scale section.

Q: Why is it called Audra?
A: Audra means 'Storm' in Old Norse. We are the calm intelligence
   inside the misinformation storm.
